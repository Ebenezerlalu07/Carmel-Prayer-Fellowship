Welcome to Our Church

Good morning, and a warm welcome to Carmel Prayer Fellowship! Whether it's your first visit or you've been with us for years, we're so happy to have you here with us.

Our story began back in 2013 with a Spirit-led prayer gathering in someone's home. Fast forward to May 2018, and that little prayer meeting blossomed into a dedicated house church with twelve members, all guided by the Holy Spirit. In 2019, we welcomed our founding pastor, Robin C. Roy, to help nurture our growth in faith, prayer, and service. Today, we're a close-knit community focused on worship and dedicated to assisting lives and families to experience the freedom of God's love.

We come together to pray for the deliverance of generations from burdens that may have weighed them down, to encourage one another in love, and to equip everyone to serve. We want to express the power of Pentecost in our lives, believing that the Holy Spirit empowers us to be bold witnesses and ignites a passion for prayer and service within us. We're eagerly praying and looking forward to the next wave of revival in our nation emerging from vibrant communities like ours, inspired by the Holy Spirit as we engage in prayer and service.

Our vision is to grow into a multicultural, multiethnic church that beautifully represents the fullness of God's family. You'll see this in our warm welcomes, uplifting worship, and the incredible stories we share.

We'd love for you to visit our church and become part of our faith family. We can't wait to see you!
